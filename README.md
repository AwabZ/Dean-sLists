# 🎯 Criteria for Achievement

- GPA Threshold: Achieve a minimum Semester GPA of 3.50.

- Credit Load: Successful completion of at least 15 credit hours for full-time students.

- Integrity: No failing grades, incomplete grades, or disciplinary actions.
